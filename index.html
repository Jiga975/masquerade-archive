About

The Igbo Masquerade Archive is an online meseum, dedicated solely for promogating the mmanwu(masquerade) heritage of the Ndigbo from the south-eastern region of Nigeria. It is aimed to showcase images, videos, and article relevant for the promotion, record-keeping and education of the present, but most importantly, the future Igbo people. Igbo Kwenu!
